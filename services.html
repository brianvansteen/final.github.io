The history of your club
The LEGO Star Wars club was founded in 2006, by Michael "R2D2" MacCrimmon.
The first meeting of the club took place on June 17, 2006.
The membership has grown from 5 at that first meeting, to more than 200 Jedis!
We host more than 10 events every year.
