The Ottawa LEGO Star Wars club is your one-stop for all things LEGO and all things Star Wars!

We start off in 2006 as a monthly meet-up to build or excahgne LEGO Star Wars items, but have grown since then.

There are now more than 200 members, and we have many dedicated events in addition to our monthly meeting.

Our club meets on the first Saturday of every month, at the Ottawa Marriott in the Canada room.

We always welcome new members, and hope to see you at our next monthly meeting!
